The motion graphics were designed to comunicate the brands function.The designs visually describes what the brand does.

Hype Fragrance

Hype is a fragrance used by sports players,athletes and sports fans.

The perfume encourages sports players to challenge their limit without worrying about bad body odour and skin irritation.The fragrance can be afforded by middle income earner and sold at retail stores.

The motion graphic advertises different sports players challenging themselves and showing their skills.The motion graphic shows how the logo builds on screen using different 3d shapes apearing on screen from different directions and rotations.

The background shows or suggests the possible result when used in different active spaces.At the end it shows a 3d design of Hype fragrance.

I used Black and Lime Greens to make the brand to look like a sports brand.



Sunshine Tours

Sunshine Tours is a adventure tourism company that is located at Eastern Cape.

The logo is designed in motion to describe the adventure tours at Tsitsikamma. Sunshine tours logo can be advertised in digital and broadcasting platforms to invite people who are looking for thrilling activities.

The tourism company serves people who enjoys adventure and who wants to experience nature while having fun.Tsitsikamma has beautiful mountains and oceans.

Sunshine tours is a perfect company to experience Eastern Cape.Sunshine tours has affordable packages for BnB's,hotels and adventurous activities.

Sunshine Tours makes sure you lack nothing to prepare for the tour and come as you are to enjoy.



Rise And Shine

Rise and Shine is a morning show that plays every weekdays. A logo motion graphic rebrand is inspired by a sunrise.

The morning show aims to update people with the latest news,sports, finance,traffic,entertainment and weather.

Rise and Shine show informs everyone who would like to know what is happening around the world with a goal to prepare for daily challenges.

Rise and Shine has been rebranded to stay relevent with the changes.

The Rise and Shine motion logo is designed to be advertised on a television and digital platforms.



Youth Day Art Festival

The event should reflect the idea of freedom,a festive feeling as well as use the landscape in and around Constitutional hill.

I had to consider the clients view on how the idea of freedom is portrayed in terms of their style,product or service.

Youth Day Art Festival is a 5 day music festival that is in Johannesburg at Constitutional hill.The event celebrates youth in June.

The motion graphic goal is to show what to expect at the event and shows how the place looks like.

The motion graphic is designed to be promoted on social media platforms and broadcasting platforms.
